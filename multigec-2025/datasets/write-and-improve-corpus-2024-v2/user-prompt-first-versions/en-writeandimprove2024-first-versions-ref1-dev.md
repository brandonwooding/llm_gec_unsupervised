After that, you can see on the top left corner, " Welcome (your name) " and a sign out button in blue, as it says, it's to sign out from your account. Below it, you'll see " W&I workbooks " , just click there to open every type of workbook that W&I has. Below it, when you close W&I workbooks, you'll see a test zone. There is the IELTS Academic test, IELTS General Training and B2 First. They are official tests of English (not certified ones). Below it, you'll see " My workbooks'. "  My workbooks " is for creating a new workbook or joining a workbook. Below it, you'll see " Create a workbook " and, as its name says, it's for creating a workbook. Below it, you'll see " Join a workbook," and that is just to join a workbook. There are even more than you thought, there're still more things to see. Below join a workbook is " Class View " . For now, that's for teachers, that helps teachers to see what their students have done. After, you'll see Progress, My account and Help centre. Progress is to see your achievements on W&I, my account is for looking at your profile with more detail, and help centre is for asking for some help, or reporting any errors. Why did I write all that text if you could practice that? Easy, because this is a review of my experience and I wanted to tell English learners that just found W&I. Anyways, taking my experience with W&I, I could just say that, there aren't as many errors as on other websites. The important things are very easily placed and very comfortable for you to find them. So, that's it. Thank you for reading learner/teacher of English. Good day.
